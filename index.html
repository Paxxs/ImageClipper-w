Image Clipper

Image Clipper boosts your learning efficiency on the iPad. Quickly segment images and copy them to the clipboard. Import images from the photo album or clipboard, draw selection areas, and the app will automatically copy the selected content. Ideal for segmenting PDF test papers and copying questions to note-taking apps like OneNote. The app retains segmented images for future use. No network connection required, ensuring privacy and security.
